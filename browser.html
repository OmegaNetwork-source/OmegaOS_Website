Wallet Connections

Connect to dApps using WalletConnect

How to Connect Wallets:

This browser uses WalletConnect to connect to wallets. No browser extensions needed!

Steps:
  1. Visit a dApp (like Uniswap, Jupiter, etc.)
  2. Click "Connect Wallet"
  3. Select "WalletConnect" from the options
  4. Scan the QR code with your mobile wallet app:
    • MetaMask Mobile - for Ethereum dApps
    • Phantom Mobile - for Solana dApps
    • Or any WalletConnect-compatible wallet
  5. Approve the connection in your mobile wallet
  6. You're connected! Your wallet works in the dApp.
